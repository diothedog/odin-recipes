Pork Chops

Description

If you've ever wondered how to get perfectly pan fried pork chops, this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • Fresh pork chops
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in pan over medium-high heat for a few minutes, until piping hot.
  2. Season pork chops with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add pork chops to pan.
  4. Cook pork chops for about three of four minutes per side before flipping.
  5. Serve with your choice of vegetable for a side.
