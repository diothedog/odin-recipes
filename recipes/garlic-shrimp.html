Garlic Shrimp

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe that is loaded with protein. I always keep some shrimp in my freezer, knowing that I am always ten to fifteen minutes away from a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Half a pound of frozen shrimp, peeled and de-veined
  • Three cloves of garlic
  • Crushed red pepper flakes
  • Sweet Spanish paprika
  • Salt
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • White rice
