Chicken Fajitas

Description

This delicious recipe for chicken fajita tacos is easy to make and gluten free if you use corn tortillas!

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Italian dressing
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Fiesta chicken fajita seasoning with MSG
  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts generously with the fajita seasoning.
  2. Marinate the chicken breasts overnight in a 50/50 mixture of Italian dressing and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Heat the tortillas in a small skillet over medium heat until lightly browned.
  4. Cook the chicken breasts over medium-high heat until cooked through.
  5. Slice the chicken into strips, cutting against the grain.
  6. Add the chicken to the tortillas and top with shredded cheese.
